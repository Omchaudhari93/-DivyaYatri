🛕 Trimbakeshwar Temple

Nashik | Jyotirling

About the Temple

Trimbakeshwar Temple is one of the 12 sacred Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, located in Nashik district, Maharashtra. The temple is famous for its unique three-faced linga representing Brahma, Vishnu, and Mahesh. It is situated at the foot of the Brahmagiri Hills and the origin of the holy Godavari River.

📍 Address

Trimbak, Nashik, Maharashtra 422212

  • Evening: 3:00 PM – 9:00 PM

🚍 How to Reach

The temple is about 30 km from Nashik City. You can reach via state transport buses, cabs, or private vehicles. Nashik is well connected by road and rail from major cities like Mumbai and Pune.
