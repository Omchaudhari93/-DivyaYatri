🛕 Tulja Bhavani Temple

Osmanabad | Shaktipeeth

About the Temple

Tulja Bhavani Temple, located in Tuljapur, Osmanabad, is one of the 51 Shaktipeethas in India. The goddess Bhavani is revered as a powerful incarnation of Shakti and the family deity of many Maharashtrian families, including the great Maratha warrior Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

📍 Address

Tuljapur, Osmanabad, Maharashtra 413601

⏰ Darshan Timings

  • Morning: 5:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • Evening: 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM

🚍 How to Reach

Tuljapur is well connected via road. Regular buses and private taxis are available from Solapur and Osmanabad. The nearest railway station is Solapur (45 km).
