About the Temple

Tulja Bhavani Temple, located in Tuljapur, Osmanabad, is one of the 51 Shaktipeethas in India. The goddess Bhavani is revered as a powerful incarnation of Shakti and the family deity of many Maharashtrian families, including the great Maratha warrior Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
